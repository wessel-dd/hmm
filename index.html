About Wyvern

We are 6 students from ROC Technovium of which 3 come from the Software Developer course and the other 3 from the Media and Design course. In this project we have been given the assignment to program a Lego EV3 robot using the programming language Python. According to the assignment the robot must do a few things: the robot must be able to follow a black line with curves in it, it must be able to do something with a red line where there is an obstacle where we have chosen to let it drive through at a higher speed, and at a green dot on the ground is the end of the track where we let it stop driving and then it makes a sound to let you know it stopped.
